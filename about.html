Our Story

A Legacy Built on Trust

Shoinik Paper & Stationeries began as a small neighborhood shop in Bogra with one simple goal: to make quality stationery accessible and affordable for students, professionals, and families. Over the years, what started as a modest counter selling notebooks and pens has grown into a trusted destination for all things paper and stationery.

Under the leadership of owner Torikul Islam, the shop has built a reputation for honesty, fair pricing, and genuine care for every customer who walks through the door. We've watched generations of students grow up shopping with us — and we wouldn't have it any other way.

Today, we stock hundreds of products across dozens of categories, from everyday school essentials to specialized office and art supplies, all while keeping the same personal touch that built our reputation.
