Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

  1. Home
  2. Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Shoinik Paper & Stationeries ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you share with us through this website. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

Since this website does not use a backend server or database, we do not store any personal information on our servers. When you place an order or contact us, the information you provide (name, phone number, address) is sent directly to our WhatsApp number and is only used to process your order or respond to your inquiry.

We may also use your browser's local storage to temporarily save your shopping cart contents and theme preference (dark/light mode) for a better browsing experience. This data stays on your own device and is never transmitted to us unless you complete an order.

3. How We Use Your Information

Any information shared with us via WhatsApp or our contact form is used solely to: process and deliver your order, respond to your inquiries, and improve our customer service. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

This website uses browser local storage (not cookies) to remember your cart items and theme preference. This data remains on your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google Maps to display our shop location and WhatsApp to receive orders and messages. These third-party services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information when you interact with them.

6. Data Security

While we do not store personal data on our own servers, we encourage you to be mindful when sharing sensitive information online. WhatsApp messages are encrypted end-to-end by WhatsApp's own security measures.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at 01724-389919 or via WhatsApp.
